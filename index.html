Where Winter Meets the Wild

Explore the pristine alpine forests and frozen peaks of Northwind Valley.

The Frozen Sanctuary

Northwind Valley is a unique high-altitude reserve where lush evergreen forests meet perpetual snow. Established to protect the fragile alpine ecosystem, it provides a habitat for cold-resilient species like the Snow Leopard, Red Panda, and Himalayan Monal.

Located in the upper reaches of the Northern Range, the sanctuary offers a misty, magical experience. Whether you enjoy winter trekking, silent nature walks among ancient pine trees, or photography in the mist, Northwind offers an escape into a serene, cold wilderness.

Major Attractions

Snow Leopard Trek

Embark on a guided winter trek to spot the elusive 'Ghost of the Mountains' in its natural icy habitat.

Glacial Falls

Witness the stunning partially frozen waterfalls surrounded by frost-covered rocks and moss.

Winter Birding

Observe rare high-altitude birds like the Snowy Owl and Golden Eagle from our silent viewing decks.
